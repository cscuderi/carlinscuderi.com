About

My name is Carlin; I'm a web developer and designer with a passion for building accessible, engaging web experiences.

I believe in small details. Technology and the way people interact with it is an extremely important part of our lives. Learning about patterns, conventions, behaviours and how to create a positive experience for a wide audience is essential. I like to design and build things that other people will really enjoy using, and pass what I've learned on to help others do the same.

I'm currently available on a part-time basis for consultation, freelance contracts, and collaboration.

  • Emphasis on accessibility, inclusiveness, and universal design
  • Desire to educate and help others
